PARTNERSHIP-BASED DISTRIBUTION MODEL

Regional Operation Structure

  • The Company will set up 7 regional offices/branches in Eastern China, Central China, Northern China, Southern China, Northwestern China, Southwestern China and Eastern China.
  • Under each of the above-mentioned regional offices/branches there will be regional/provincial quasipartners/dealers;
  • Under the above regional/provincial quasi partners there will be local quasi partners/dealers;
  • Under the above local quasi partners/dealers there will be authorized/licensed distributors.
  • All regional or local quasi-partners/dealers/authorized or licensed distributors, after achieving the annual performance goals and getting approved by the Company, have the chance to be promoted to be our partners and to participate and manage Sci-Gene’s branches/offices as well as their businesses.Namely: Sci-GeneRegi onal branches/offices, Regional or Provincial quasi-partners/dealers, Local quasi-partners/dealers, Licensed/authorized distributors.
  • The Partners/quasi-partners/dealers/distributors should acknowledge the advantages of Sci-Gene’s products, agree to our operational principles and cooperation model, be aware of the importance of brand operation and be fully in charge of marketing and sales, technical support and installation services (The installation and conditioning costs are included in the discounted prices) within their respective local markets.

SALES STANDARDS

Uniform market guidance price:

The partners, quasi partners/dealers and distributors should rigorously observe the market guidance price set by the Company without arbitrarily mark up or mark down the price and are forbidden to disrupt the market price of ETL products.

Regional Exclusivity:

The partners, quasi partners/dealers and distributors shall stringently observe regional exclusivity, namely, they should not sell ETL products outside their assigned sales area.
Selling ETL products outside their assigned sales area will be deemed as violation of the exclusivity principle regardless of the price of such products.
And penalties will be imposed on such violations: the Company has the right to increase the wholesale price for those who violated the regional exclusivity principle and if the situation is serious, the Company may revoke their licenses and the breaching party shall also bear the damages and other related costs.

Rebate:

For partners, quasi partners/dealers and distributors who report robust sales performance that year, The company will, after reviewing offer them channel fee or rebate at a certain proportion which can be used for the next year’s payment for goods or be offered in cash.

Reward for exceeding sales goals:

In the case that the quasi partners/dealers and authorized distributors sell 50% more than their respective sales goals (sets of products), they can enjoy 1% lower than the discounted price next year and the same goes for each of the following years, but the sum of such further discount percentage each year shall not exceed 3% and the final discounted price shall not be lower than 55% of the market guidance price.
备注:The discounts only apply to basic ETL system devices, excluding optional end devices (the prices of custom end devices shall be subject to the then-current quoted prices)

Penalty for falling short of the goals:

Whether the sales goals have been achieved can be determined in two ways:
1. by the amount of sets of products agreed upon or
2. by converting the sales amount to the standard market guidance price.
For those who have not achieved the sales goals of that year, the wholesale price for them will be increased pro rata, and provided that there exist serious issues in their sales performance, the Company, after reviewing, may terminate the cooperation and sales agreements and may also revoke their licenses to sell ETL products after taking inventory and settling accounts.
