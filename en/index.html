About Us

Sci-Gene is a leading smart home control system solution provider and data platform service operator. Having been dedicated to the commercialization of high-tech projects, and the study and production of high-end smart home products, we are one of the earliest enterprises in smart home industry.
Started from 2002, with 15 years of professional R&D as well as 12 years of practical experience, in partnership with Jianyi Investment, Shanghai Sci-Gene Technology Co., Ltd was officially established in 2017, designed to provide integrated smart home solutions that cover design, installation, service and operation.
